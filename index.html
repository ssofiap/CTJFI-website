Come visit us!

Join us in our weekly Sunday service at 10am* (or 3pm, please visit our Facebook page or our Instagram page for more information concerning our schedule.)

Our weekly Sunday service is held at the Temple de Malagnou, Chemin Rieu 3, 1208 Geneva.

If you are taking the bus TPG, you may take the bus 5, 11, or 25 and get off at the stop "Rieu". From there, you'll have to walk for a bit.
